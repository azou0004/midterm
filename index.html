Life in the Ocean

The ocean is home to millions of species and is one of the most important ecosystems on Earth. Marine animals such as turtles, dolphins, and coral reefs create a rich and diverse environment.

Scientists continue to explore the oceans to discover new species and understand how marine life affects our planet. Protecting oceans is essential for the future of Earth.

Interesting Ocean Facts

  • Oceans cover more than 70% of Earth.
  • The Pacific Ocean is the largest ocean.
  • More than 200,000 marine species have been identified.
  • Coral reefs support thousands of ocean animals.
